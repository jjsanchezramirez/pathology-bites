## Strategic Notes
- **100% high-yield content** due to limited question allocation requiring maximum educational efficiency
- **Emphasis on ischemic heart disease** (MI dating, atherosclerosis) as most commonly encountered cardiac pathology
- **Cardiomyopathy differential prioritized** as classic pathology topic with high clinical relevance
- **Vasculitis coverage focused** on most important large vessel (GCA) and small-medium vessel differentials
- **Cardiac tumors streamlined** to myxoma only as the most classic and commonly tested entity
- **Excluded rare conditions** (Marfan syndrome, specific hereditary cardiomyopathies, cardiac transplant rejection)
- **Combined related inflammatory conditions** into differential diagnosis questions for educational efficiency
- **Normal anatomy excluded** to maximize space for pathologic conditions
- **Balanced core vs. advanced content** reflecting that cardiovascular pathology spans skill levels
